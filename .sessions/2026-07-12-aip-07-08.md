## 💡 Session idea

AIP-07 existed because the refresh call is a per-handler convention
with no structural backstop: six write-acking handlers in one module,
four remembered `_refresh_settings_page`, two didn't, and no test or
checker noticed for a full audit cycle. A tiny module-scoped guard
(test or tools/ checker) asserting every handler in `_HANDLERS` whose
success arm writes via `_write_setting` also awaits
`_refresh_settings_page` before its success Reply would make the next
forgotten refresh a red test instead of a sim-lab finding.
