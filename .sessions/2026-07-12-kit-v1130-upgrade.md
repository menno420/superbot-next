## 💡 Session idea

The upgrade-distribution playbook's step-6 "check the PREVIOUS upgrade
PR's report for dropped template-delta flags" is manual archaeology every
wave. Idea: have `upgrade` itself carry forward unapplied
template-improved/diverged rows from the report it is about to overwrite
into a "still outstanding from vN" section — the engine has both files in
hand at exactly the right moment; a kit-side change (propose upstream),
not repo-side.
