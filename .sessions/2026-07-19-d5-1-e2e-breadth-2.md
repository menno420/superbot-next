## 💡 Idea

Add a `providers: list[ProviderCapture]` sink to `RealAdapterRecorder` that
records each `FieldsBlock` provider's `(ctx, rows)` as it fires — so a
provider-fed panel (treasury, and every future one) gets a one-line assertion
that the DB read actually ran and produced the rows the embed shows, closing the
gap between "the embed says 0" and "the provider queried the store and got 0"
without a test hand-calling the store. The natural companion to the
presenter/emitter swaps: a third recording seam for the data-provider band.
